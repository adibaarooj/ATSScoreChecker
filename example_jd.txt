About the Role

Assist in designing and implementing data-driven solutions to solve business problems

Build and evaluate machine learning models under guidance from senior data scientists

Collaborate with cross-functional teams including engineering, product, and business stakeholders
Contribute to data pipelines, dashboards, and reporting tools
Document workflows, methodologies, and findings clearly
